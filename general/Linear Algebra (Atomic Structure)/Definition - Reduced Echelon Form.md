A matrix is in **reduced echelon form** if it satisfies the following properties:

a.  It is in [[Definition - Echelon Form|echelon form]].
b.  The leading entry (pivot) of each nonzero row is 1.
c.  Each leading 1 is the only nonzero entry in its column.
