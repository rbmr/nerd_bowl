There are three types of **elementary row operations** that can be performed on an augmented matrix to produce an [[Definition - Equivalent Systems|equivalent system]]:

a.  **Replacement:** Replace one row by the sum of itself and a multiple of another row.
b.  **Interchange:** Interchange two rows.
c.  **Scaling:** Multiply all entries in one row by a nonzero constant.
