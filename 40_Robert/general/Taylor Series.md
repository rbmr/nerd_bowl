- The Taylor series of a function $f$ centered at a point $a$ is given by the formula:$$f(x) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{f^{(n)}(a)}{n!} (x-a)^n$$
- The Maclaurin series is a Taylor series centered at $a=0$:$$f(x) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{f^{(n)}(0)}{n!} x^n$$
